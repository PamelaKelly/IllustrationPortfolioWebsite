Song of the Sea - Illustration Inspiration

'Song of the Sea' is an local Irish production. Truly inspiring illustration & story. I recently saw an exhibition about the production process of the film at The Ark, Temple Bar. It was incredible to see the character work as it progressed as well as the truly stunning concept art. I found it extremely interesting that there were a number of different styles of drawing among the various people working on the film, and yet the overall finished product is cohesive.
